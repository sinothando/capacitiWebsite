About Capaciti

Established in 2010, CAPACITI, part of the UVU Africa Group, is at the forefront of preparing young individuals for the tech sector. CAPACITI has emerged as one of South Africa’s leading digital talent accelerators, annually training and placing approximately 1000 unemployed youth. As a non-profit organisation, CAPACITI leverages partner investments to deliver demand-driven, targeted technical training that positively impacts lives.

Mission

CAPACITI aims to democratize tech careers and foster inclusive societies in Africa, viewing quality education as essential for a diverse and growing digital economy.

Vision

We aim to empower digitally marginalized youth with technical skills and work readiness, focusing on meaningful career opportunities beyond just job acquisition.

Ethos

CAPACITI prioritizes respect and excellence, offering specialized training for a future-ready workforce and creating opportunities in the digital economy through constant innovation.

Kate Maakane, Junior Digital Administrator

When I went to my interview at CAPACITI, I wore flip flops, the only shoes I had at the time. I thought they would judge me but instead, they saw my potential. Their recognition lit a fire in me and for the first time, I didn’t feel invisible. Through hands-on experience in Salesforce and exposure to real business processes, I was challenged to grow.

"I Walked In With Flip-Flops. I Walked Out With Purpose."

Since joining CAPACITI, I’ve been surrounded by a supportive team of mentors that even offered me counselling when I needed it most. I started this journey in flip-flops, unsure if I belonged. Today, I walk into boardrooms as a Junior Digital Administrator—proof that growth starts when someone believes in you, and you begin to believe it too.

Siphelo Nyandana , DevOps Engineer

When I first started at CAPACITI everything was moving fast, and I couldn’t keep up. I found coding confusing, and I was intimidated because all my peers were fluent in coding language. Luckily, I was with a supportive team that helped me to keep up. My mentors also supported me, and I stopped feeling alone and became part of CAPACITI’s family.

"Grateful to be part of CAPACITI’s supportive family "

I was excited when CAPACITI helped me land a job as an Intern at Loop because finding a tech job is not easy. When I worked at Loop, I was able to show off the tech skills I learnt at CAPACITI and to continue learning from experienced tech managers.
