About Capaciti

Established in 2010, CAPACITI, part of the UVU Africa Group, is at the forefront of preparing young individuals for the tech sector. CAPACITI has emerged as one of South Africa‚Äôs leading digital talent accelerators, annually training and placing approximately 1000 unemployed youth. CAPACITI is dedicated to fostering the inclusive growth of the digital economy by creating a pipeline of future-fit, skilled individuals equipped with relevant digital skills aligned with industry demand. Since its inception, CAPACITI has been refining solutions that aim to economically integrate talent, by providing scalable digital skills training and preparing them for the evolving workplace of the future. As a non-profit organisation, CAPACITI leverages partner investments to deliver demand-driven, targeted technical training that positively impacts lives.

Mission

CAPACITI aims to democratize tech careers and foster inclusive societies in Africa, viewing quality education as essential for a diverse and growing digital economy.

Vision

We aim to empower digitally marginalized youth with technical skills and work readiness, focusing on meaningful career opportunities beyond just job acquisition.

Ethos

CAPACITI prioritizes respect and excellence, offering specialized training for a future-ready workforce and creating opportunities in the digital economy through constant innovation.

Our Story

The journey of building Africa's digital future, one skill at a time

2018

The Beginning

Capaciti was founded with a vision to bridge the digital skills gap across Africa. Starting in Cape Town, we identified the urgent need for practical, industry-relevant training that could transform lives and communities.

2019

First Partnerships

We formed strategic partnerships with leading technology companies and educational institutions, creating pathways for our graduates to enter the digital economy with confidence and competence.

2020

Digital Transformation

The global pandemic accelerated digital adoption across Africa. We quickly adapted our programs to meet the new reality, launching online learning platforms and expanding our reach to rural communities.

2021

Continental Expansion

Our success in South Africa led to expansion across the continent. We established training centers in Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, and other key markets, adapting our curriculum to local needs and opportunities.

2024

Leading the Future

Today, Capaciti stands as a leading force in Africa's digital transformation. With over 5,000 graduates and partnerships across 12 countries, we continue to innovate and expand our impact.
